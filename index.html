About Me

I have a strong passion for technology, particularly in the field of instrumentation, where I find joy in designing and developing systems that play a crucial role in various industries. My academic journey has equipped me with a solid foundation in subjects like control systems, sensors and transducers, signal processing, and industrial automation.

These experiences have not only enhanced my technical skills but also improved my teamwork and project management abilities.

I am enthusiastic about applying my knowledge to real-world problems and making a meaningful contribution to society.

Outside of academics, I enjoy to play. These activities help me maintain a balanced lifestyle and cultivate creativity and problem-solving skills that are beneficial in both personal and professional aspects of life.

Blind Stick using Arduino

Arduino-powered Blind Stick, a groundbreaking assistive device for the visually impaired. Combining state-of-the-art IR sensor technology with an Arduino microcontroller, this compact tool detects obstacles in real-time, providing immediate auditory feedback to users. Lightweight and user-friendly, our Blind Stick enhances mobility and safety, empowering individuals to navigate with confidence in diverse environments. Its customizable features and seamless integration exemplify our commitment to innovation in assistive technology, ensuring greater independence and inclusivity for all. Join us in revolutionizing accessibility and fostering a world where everyone can navigate with ease and dignity.
